FAQ

Why 90 seconds?

The 90-second constraint forces filmmakers to distill their stories to their absolute essence. Every frame must earn its place. This format celebrates the power of brevity and creative constraint in storytelling.

Is there a submission fee?

Yes, there is a modest submission fee to cover administrative costs and ensure serious entries. Early bird submissions receive a discounted rate. Fee waivers are available for filmmakers facing financial hardship.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept MP4, MOV, and AVI formats. Films should be submitted in 1080p resolution minimum. Files must not exceed 2GB in size.

Can I submit multiple films?

Yes, you may submit multiple films. Each submission requires a separate entry fee. However, each filmmaker can only have one film selected for the festival screening.

When will I be notified if my film is selected?

Selected filmmakers will be notified by email within one week of the submission deadline. All entrants will receive confirmation of their submission status.
