FAQ

Why 90 seconds?

The 90-second constraint forces filmmakers to distill their stories to their absolute essence. Every frame must earn its place. This format celebrates the power of brevity and creative constraint in storytelling.

Is there a submission fee?

No, submission is completely free. We believe great films should be judged on their merit, not on your budget.
